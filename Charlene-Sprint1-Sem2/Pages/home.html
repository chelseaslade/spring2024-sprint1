About Gary

Gary grew up in Bonavista, where his mother and grandmother were huge influences on his cooking style. Gary attended the Culinary Arts School of Toronto, where he graduated top of his class, before pursuing a diploma in Tourism and Hospitality Management from College of the North Atlantic.

He opened his first Gary Blue's restaurant in 2011 and has been expanding the business across Atlantic Canada ever since. He currently lives in St. John's with his partner, Simon, and their dog, Garfunkel.
